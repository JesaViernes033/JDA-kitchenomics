Ginisang Upo at Togue

I hope you give these dishes a try. They're easy to make, budget-friendly, and tasty as they're nutritious.

Preparation Time: 5 mins.

Cooking Time: 35 mins.

Total Time: 40 mins.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup tawgi (togue/mung bean sprouts), wash and cleaned
  • 1 cup kalubay (upo), sliced
  • 3/4 cup Pork slices
  • 1/2 Shrimps, with "shells"
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Salt and Pepper (or pork bouillon cubes)
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. In a heated pan, put pork slices and fry it in its own oil.
  2. ute in garlic, onion and tomatoes then add kalubay and togue.
  3. Add water and season with salt and pepper (or broth cubes.).
  4. Boil and simmer until the pork and vegetables are cooked.
  5. Add shrimps and boil for 2-3 minutes or until it turns orange
  6. Remove from heat and serve.
